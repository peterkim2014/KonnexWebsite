Frequently asked questions

What should I do if I lose access to my Lotus crypto wallet?

If you find yourself unable to access your Lotus wallet, follow the instructions provided by Lotus to recover your wallet. If you don't have a backup phrase or still can't access your wallet, reach out to Lotus's customer support for further assistance. They're there to help you navigate through any issues you may encounter.

Can I use the same Lotus wallet for multiple cryptocurrencies?

Yes, Lotus wallets support multiple cryptocurrencies, thanks to Polkadot's interoperability features. Ensure your chosen wallet supports the specific cryptocurrencies you intend to manage.

How can I ensure the security of my Lotus crypto wallet?

Keeping your Lotus wallet secure is essential. Never share your wallet's private keys or recovery phrases with anyone, and keep them stored safely offline.

What should I do if I forget my Lotus crypto wallet password?

Lotus provides a password recovery option through security questions. Once you've successfully answered the questions, you'll be able to reset your password and regain access to your wallet. If you encounter any difficulties during the recovery process, don't hesitate to reach out to Lotus's customer support for further assistance.
